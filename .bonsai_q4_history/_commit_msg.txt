Batched setQ4Buffer from 3 individual ObjC message sends into a single setBuffers:offsets:withRange: call. The same MTLBuffer is bound at 3 consecutive indices (nibbles, scales, biases) with different offsets. Saves ~400 ObjC calls per decode step (7 Q4 bindings per block × 28 blocks × 2 saved calls each). Result: small but consistent improvement from 223.3 to ~223.7 tok/s (0.2%). P99 improved from 4711 to ~4660μs.
